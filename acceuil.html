History of Well Being Advocate

Well Being Advocate was founded by Dr. Ime Charline,
a laboratory technician and mental health advocate,
with a mission to support mental health awareness, empower individuals,
and provide resources for psychological resilience.
The organization was established out of a deep commitment to destigmatizing mental health issues,
particularly among individuals living with HIV,
and to create a safe space for mental well-being.

×




Mission

Well Being Advocate is committed to promoting mental health awareness,
providing accessible counseling and training services,
and empowering individuals to become mental health advocates.
Our goal is to reduce stigma, offer professional support,
and create sustainable mental health solutions for communities.

Our Vision

To build a world where mental health is prioritized, supported, and accessible to all,
ensuring that every individual has the resources and knowledge to lead a mentally
resilient and fulfilling life.

Vision for the Future


Well Being Advocate aims to continue expanding its impact by:
Reaching underserved populations with mental health resources
Developing structured mental health programs for various communities
Influencing mental health policies at local and national levels
Integrating mental health support into everyday life through training, workshops, and advocacy campaigns

×







Objectives


• ✔ Provide professional mental health counseling and support services.
• ✔ Train individuals to become certified mental health counselors and advocates.
• ✔ Raise awareness and reduce stigma around mental health in communities.
• ✔ Develop sustainable business models to support mental health initiatives.
• ✔ Partner with organizations to integrate mental health into education and workplaces.
• ✔ Establish a crisis intervention system to provide emergency support.
• ✔ Create a global network of mental health professionals and advocates.
• ✔ Promote mental health research and innovation to improve services and outcomes.

Key Milestones

Foundation and Early Mission:
Well Being Advocate began as a mental health awareness initiative,
focusing on empowerment, resilience, and advocacy.
Expansion of Programs: Over time, the organization expanded its outreach,
developing programs such as:
Mental Health Ambassador Training Program ("Empowering Voices")
Workshops and Seminars on mental health,
resilience,
and well-being
Youth-focused Initiatives on mental health education
Art Therapy Workshops and other innovative healing approaches
Training and Capacity Building: The organization launched
'Becoming a Mental Health Counselor in 3 Months',
an initiative to train and equip individuals to provide mental health support.
Community Building:
Well Being Advocate envisions creating a strong community
of mental health counselors and advocates to continue spreading awareness and providing support.
